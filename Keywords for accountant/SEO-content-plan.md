# NJ's Accounting — 30-Day SEO Content Plan

**Derived from:** `keyword-clusters.csv` (253 keywords, 14 clusters).
**Scope:** the 6 blog posts to write in the first month post-launch. Everything else in the CSV either supports these or gets ignored.
**Voice guardrail:** every post reads plain-spoken, calm, jargon-free. Never sound like a stuffy accounting firm.

---

## Publishing order (why this sequence)

The order is deliberate — highest commercial intent first, then education, then empathy, then differentiation. Each post also **internally links** to at least one of the others, so ranking on any one lifts the whole cluster.

## Post-by-post brief

### Post 1 — "Do you actually need an accountant for your small business?"

- **Also covers:** `do i need an llc for a small business`, `does my small business need an llc`
- **Working headlines:**
  1. Do you actually need an accountant for your small business? (An honest answer.)
  2. When a small business needs an accountant — and when it doesn't
- **Outline:** 3 signs you're ready → 3 signs you're not → the DIY-vs-hire cost math → what an accountant *actually* does for a $299/mo business
- **Word count:** 1,200–1,500
- **CTA:** book a free 15-min discovery call
- **Internal links:** → /pricing, → Post 2 (Bookkeeper vs Accountant)

## What we're NOT writing in month 1 (deliberately)

- **Cluster F (solopreneur/1099):** volume is there, but posts overlap heavily with Cluster A. Write these month 2 if analytics show side-hustle traffic converting.
- **Cluster H (state-specific sales tax):** these get satisfied by state gov websites and are hard to outrank. Fold key ones into the Shopify post (Post 3).
- **State/local geographic queries, career/student queries, banking product research, gaming/social account noise:** 79% of the raw keyword file is one of these. Never target them.

---

## How we'll know it's working (30/60/90 day)

- **Day 30 checkpoint:** all 6 posts live, indexed in Google Search Console, sitemap resubmitted.
- **Day 60:** first ranking data. Realistic expectation — posts 2, 4, 6 rank first (lower KD); posts 1, 3, 5 take longer (higher intent = more competition).
- **Day 90:** first inbound leads from organic. Cluster A + C are the ones most likely to convert; instrument /contact and /pricing with UTM to see which post drove each lead.
- **Trigger to iterate:** if any post gets impressions but zero clicks by day 60, rewrite the meta description. If any post gets clicks but zero form fills by day 90, rewrite the CTA block.
