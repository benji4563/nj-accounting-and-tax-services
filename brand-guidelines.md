## 4. Voice & Tone

### Voice principles
1. **Short sentences. Verbs first.**
   Not: *"It is our belief that in order to maximize your tax outcomes…"*
   Yes: *"We find deductions most accountants miss."*

2. **Say the small-business word.**
   Not: *"general ledger reconciliation."* Yes: *"we keep your books clean."*
   Not: *"Q4 filing cadence."* Yes: *"tax season."*

3. **Numbers are specific.**
   Not: *"trusted by many small businesses."*
   Yes: *"We've saved 47 small businesses $2.3M in taxes since 2019."*

4. **Empathy first. Competence second. Credentials third.**
   Lead with the client's problem. Then show you can solve it. Only then mention the CPA badge.

5. **Never scare-sell.**
   No: *"The IRS is coming for you!"*
   Yes: *"Most small-business audits are triggered by three simple mistakes. We make sure yours never has them."*

### Voice examples

**Do ✓**
> *You've got a business to run. Payroll to make. Customers to keep happy. The last thing you need is a shoebox full of receipts and a tax form you don't understand. That's what we're here for.*

**Don't ✗**
> *Our team of highly-qualified tax professionals leverages best-in-class methodologies to deliver comprehensive fiscal solutions for the modern small-business enterprise.*

### Tone by context

| Context | Tone dial |
|---|---|
| Homepage hero | Warm, reassuring, direct |
| Pricing page | Transparent, matter-of-fact, no fluff |
| Services page | Confident, specific, practical |
| Contact form | Friendly, low-friction, human |
| Post-audit / bad news | Calm, factual, solution-first |
| Email newsletter | Useful, brief, "here's what changed and what to do about it" |

## 5. Colour System

**The signature is a bold, warm, unexpected triad: deep Aubergine, warm Persimmon, and soft Lilac Mist.** No corporate navy. No safe gray. Colours that feel *human, premium, and creative* — because that's what NJ actually is.

### Primary palette

| Name | Hex | RGB | Where it's used |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Aubergine** | `#2C1E3F` | 44, 30, 63 | Headings, primary text, footer bg, reversed sections |
| **Persimmon** | `#EA5A3D` | 234, 90, 61 | Primary CTAs, signature accent, key numbers, underline swoosh |
| **Lilac Mist** | `#C4B0DC` | 196, 176, 220 | Ambient hero glow, image-mask backgrounds, decorative washes |
| **Cream** | `#F4EAD5` | 244, 234, 213 | Warm background (default page bg) |
| **Ivory** | `#FDFAF2` | 253, 250, 242 | Card / alternate section backgrounds |

### Support palette

| Name | Hex | Where it's used |
|---|---|---|
| **Graphite** | `#221E2E` | Body copy (aubergine-tinted charcoal for cohesion) |
| **Sage** | `#758F73` | Success states, growth signals, subtle icons |
| **Fog** | `#B4AFC0` | Dividers, muted text, disabled states (dusty lavender-gray) |
| **Blush** | `#EFD9C9` | Even softer alt section — warmer than Cream |
| **Warning** | `#B44E2A` | Errors, warnings (deeper rust — never fire-engine red) |

### Contrast (WCAG check)

| Combo | Ratio | Verdict |
|---|---|---|
| Aubergine on Cream | ~14:1 | AAA ✓ |
| Graphite on Cream | ~13:1 | AAA ✓ |
| Cream on Aubergine | ~14:1 | AAA ✓ |
| Persimmon on Aubergine | ~4.9:1 | AA text / AAA large ✓ |
| Persimmon on Cream | ~3.2:1 | AA large only — use for buttons/large text, never body copy |
| Aubergine on Lilac Mist | ~8:1 | AAA ✓ |

### Usage rules

- **Aubergine** is the workhorse dark. Use for every heading and body text (via Graphite tint).
- **Persimmon** is **precious**. One dominant use per screen — usually the primary CTA. Overuse kills its power.
- **Lilac Mist** is the *atmosphere*. Use as radial gradients behind hero images, in soft washes, in decorative shapes. Never as text.
- **Cream** is the default page background. **Never pure white** — cream signals warmth, keeps the brand distinctive.
- **Blush** and **Ivory** alternate as section backgrounds for vertical rhythm.
- **Never** invent a colour that isn't in this system. If you need something new, add it here first.

### Signature colour combos (approved & recommended)

- **Hero:** Cream bg + Aubergine headline + Persimmon CTA + Lilac Mist glow behind image
- **Pricing card highlight tier:** Aubergine bg + Ivory text + Persimmon CTA button
- **Testimonial section:** Blush bg + Aubergine text
- **Footer:** Aubergine bg + Cream text + Persimmon on hover states

## 6. Typography

Two families. That's it.

### Type stack

| Role | Font | Weights we use | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Display / Headings** | **Fraunces** | 400, 500, 600, and 500 italic | Google Fonts (free) |
| **Body / UI** | **Inter** | 400, 500, 600 | Google Fonts (free) |
| **Script emphasis (optional)** | **Caveat** | 500 | Google Fonts (free) — used *sparingly* for hand-drawn underlines and one-word emphasis (see §9 Signature Devices) |

**Why Fraunces:** a modern variable serif with warmth and personality — not stiff Times New Roman, not fussy Playfair. Feels premium AND human.
**Why Inter:** the most legible sans on the internet. Neutral, workhorse, no ego.
**Why Caveat (sparingly):** to add a handmade, human touch on hero headlines — the italicised emphasis on one or two key words per hero, echoing the underline-swoosh device.

### Type scale (base 16px, ratio 1.25 — modular)

| Token | Size (desktop) | Size (mobile) | Line-height | Weight | Use for |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `display` | 64px | 44px | 1.05 | Fraunces 500 | Homepage hero only |
| `h1` | 44px | 34px | 1.1 | Fraunces 500 | Page titles |
| `h2` | 34px | 28px | 1.15 | Fraunces 500 | Section titles |
| `h3` | 24px | 20px | 1.25 | Fraunces 500 | Sub-sections |
| `h4` | 20px | 18px | 1.3 | Inter 600 | Card titles |
| `body-lg` | 18px | 17px | 1.55 | Inter 400 | Lead paragraphs |
| `body` | 16px | 16px | 1.6 | Inter 400 | Default body |
| `body-sm` | 14px | 14px | 1.55 | Inter 400 | Meta, captions |
| `label` | 13px | 13px | 1.4 | Inter 500 (uppercase, +2% tracking) | Small labels |

### Typography rules

- Letter-spacing on Fraunces headings: `-0.015em` (slight tighten — feels premium)
- Letter-spacing on `label`: `+0.04em` uppercase
- Max line length on body copy: **~68 characters** (roughly `max-w-prose` in Tailwind)
- Never use more than 2 weights per screen
- Never italicise body copy for emphasis — use weight instead
- **Italic Fraunces is reserved** for the one or two "emphasis words" per hero headline that receive the underline-swoosh device

## 7. Spacing & Layout

**Grid:** 8px base unit. Every margin, padding, gap is a multiple of 8.

**Common spacing tokens:**
`4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96, 128` (px)

**Section vertical rhythm:**
- Desktop: `96px` top/bottom padding per section
- Mobile: `64px` top/bottom padding

**Container widths:**
- Content: `max-w-6xl` (1152px)
- Prose: `max-w-2xl` (672px)
- Full-bleed hero: `max-w-7xl` (1280px)

**Corner radius:**
- Buttons, inputs: `10px` (slightly softer than v1)
- Cards: `16px`
- Modal, image containers: `20px`
- **Hero image mask:** organic asymmetric blob (see §9 Visual Signature) — not a rectangle
- Never fully-rounded pills for CTAs (too "startup-y") — square-ish with 10px radius reads more premium

---

## 8. Iconography

- **Library:** [Lucide](https://lucide.dev) — clean, consistent, free
- Stroke width: `1.5px` (default is 2 — feels heavy)
- Size defaults: `20px` inline, `24px` UI, `32px` feature blocks
- Colour: Aubergine for feature icons, Graphite for UI icons, Persimmon for CTAs and active states
- **No emoji icons anywhere.** Ever.
- **No decorative icons.** Every icon must communicate.

## 9. Visual Signature (Hero Pattern + Glow + Script)

This is what makes an NJ page *unmistakably NJ*, borrowed and adapted from a modern reference direction Ben approved.

### 9.1 The hero pattern

Split hero: text on the left, imagery on the right.

- **Left column (60%):**
  - Big Fraunces headline (`display` size), 2–3 lines
  - **One or two "emphasis words"** in italic Fraunces or Caveat script, with a hand-drawn **Persimmon underline swoosh** beneath
  - Short subhead in `body-lg`
  - One Persimmon CTA button
  - Optional: small "15+ years experience" or credibility line
- **Right column (40%):**
  - Photograph of a real person / small-business context (see §11 Imagery)
  - Image masked in an **organic asymmetric blob** shape (soft, hand-drawn feel — never a perfect circle or rectangle)
  - **Lilac Mist radial glow** placed *behind* the image, extending 20–30% beyond its edges — softly diffused (blur ~120px), 40% opacity
  - Optional: floating small element (a stat card, a testimonial quote card) overlapping the image edge for depth

### 9.2 The signature underline swoosh

- Hand-drawn SVG underline sitting beneath 1–2 emphasis words in a headline
- Colour: Persimmon `#EA5A3D`
- Stroke: `2.5–3px`, `stroke-linecap: round`
- Path: slightly imperfect, not a straight line — think a single confident pen stroke with a soft curve
- Animation (optional): draws in over 500ms `ease-out` on first scroll into view

### 9.3 Ambient glow washes

Beyond the hero, use soft radial Lilac Mist glows sparingly:
- Behind large feature imagery
- Behind pricing highlight tier
- **Never** on text-heavy sections (would reduce readability)

### 9.4 Organic shapes

For image masks and decorative shapes:
- Asymmetric blob paths (not circles, not rectangles)
- Same "shape family" reused site-wide (build 2–3 blob SVGs and reuse)
- Subtle drop shadow: `0 30px 60px rgba(44, 30, 63, 0.15)`

## 11. UI Component Patterns

### Buttons

**Primary CTA** (one per screen)
- Background: Persimmon `#EA5A3D`
- Text: Cream `#FDFAF2`, Inter 500, 15px
- Padding: `14px 26px`
- Radius: 10px
- Hover: darken 8% + subtle lift (2px translateY) + soft Persimmon glow shadow
- Transition: 180ms ease-out

**Secondary CTA**
- Background: transparent
- Border: 1.5px Aubergine
- Text: Aubergine, Inter 500
- Hover: fills with Aubergine, text becomes Cream

**Tertiary / text link**
- Persimmon text with a Persimmon underline
- Underline animates in on hover (200ms `ease-out`)

### Inputs
- Background: Ivory
- Border: 1.5px Fog
- Focus: border becomes Persimmon, subtle Persimmon glow shadow
- Radius: 10px
- Padding: `12px 16px`
- Label: `label` token above the input, Aubergine

### Cards
- Background: Ivory `#FDFAF2`, or Blush `#EFD9C9` for warmer alternates
- Border: none
- Shadow: `0 1px 2px rgba(44, 30, 63, 0.04), 0 12px 32px rgba(44, 30, 63, 0.08)`
- Radius: 16px
- Padding: 32px
- Optional hover: 4px translateY + shadow deepens

### Sections
- Alternate: Cream → Ivory → Blush → Cream (subtle warm rhythm)
- Never more than 3 background tones per page
- Aubergine sections used only for high-drama moments (footer, CTA-drop section, hero of secondary pages)

---

## 12. Motion

- **Purpose over decoration.** Every animation must have a reason.
- **Duration:** 150–300ms for micro-interactions, 400–600ms for larger transitions
- **Easing:** `ease-out` (feels natural, matches user intent) — never `linear`, never bouncy
- **On scroll:** fade-in + 8px translateY, once, no infinite loops
- **Signature underline swoosh:** draw-in animation on hero, 500ms `ease-out`, once
- **Persimmon CTA hover:** soft glow bloom, 200ms
- **Never:** parallax hero images, floating money icons, animated pie charts on the homepage, cursor-following blobs

## 14. Accessibility Baseline

- All text meets **WCAG AA** (large text at 3:1, normal at 4.5:1). AAA where feasible.
- Focus states visible on every interactive element (2px Persimmon outline, 2px offset)
- Interactive elements minimum 44×44px tap target
- All images: descriptive `alt` text
- Form labels always visible (never placeholder-only)
- No text embedded in images
- Semantic HTML — headings in order, landmarks present
- The underline swoosh is purely decorative (`aria-hidden`); the emphasis it creates must not carry meaning that isn't already in the text

## 15. Do's and Don'ts (Quick Reference)

### Do
- ✓ Use plain English. Read every sentence out loud — if you wouldn't say it, cut it.
- ✓ Lead with the client's problem, not NJ's credentials.
- ✓ Show real numbers wherever possible.
- ✓ Use Persimmon sparingly — one dominant CTA per screen.
- ✓ Use the Lilac Mist glow behind hero imagery — it's the signature move.
- ✓ Use the italic + underline-swoosh on 1–2 words in hero headlines, no more.
- ✓ Keep spacing generous. Empty space builds trust.

### Don't
- ✗ Use jargon (leverage, synergy, holistic, comprehensive, best-in-class)
- ✗ Use emoji icons or generic gradient backgrounds (purple→pink SaaS clichés)
- ✗ Use handshake/skyscraper/gold-coin imagery
- ✗ Add "AI-powered", "cutting-edge", "revolutionary" — NJ is human and reliable, that's the pitch
- ✗ Use pure white backgrounds — always Cream or Ivory
- ✗ Use navy or corporate gray anywhere
- ✗ Invent colours or fonts outside this system
- ✗ Overdo the script/italic — one moment of hand-drawn per page, max
- ✗ Cram three headlines into one section — one big idea per section
